Sriracha Scrambled Egg

Description

An excellent, spicy, quick-and-easy breakfast with simple ingredients. Serve with toast and hash brown potatoes for a complete meal.

The recipe below is for 1 serving. Adjust the ingredients according to number of servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon half-and-half
  • Salt
  • Ground pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl with half-and-half, sriracha, salt, and pepper until smooth
  2. Melt butter in nonstick pan over medium-low heat, tilting the pan to cover surface entirely with butter
  3. Cook egg mixture in pan, stirring to slowly scramble eggs, until the eggs are fully set (in 3 to 5 minutes)
